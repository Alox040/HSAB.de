Design a modern, trustworthy website for a German company providing 24-hour senior home care services.
The company name is “Hanseatische Senioren – Alltagsbegleitung”.

The service focuses on vermittlung von osteuropäischen Betreuungskräften für Senioren, allowing elderly people in Germany to stay in their own homes while receiving support with daily activities.

The design should reflect a Hanseatic / North German style: calm, trustworthy, modest, and professional. Avoid flashy colors or overly modern startup aesthetics.

Target audience:

Adults aged 40–65 searching care solutions for their parents

Seniors themselves

Families looking for trustworthy long-term home care

Design style:

Nordic / North German aesthetic

Warm, calm, trustworthy

Minimalist but friendly

Large readable typography (senior friendly)

High contrast and accessible UI

Soft colors instead of bright white

Suggested color palette:

Dark navy / maritime blue

Soft sand / warm beige

Muted light gray

Small accents in warm copper or dark red

Imagery style:

Natural and authentic scenes of seniors at home

Everyday moments (walking, tea, conversation)

Avoid hospital or clinical imagery

Not too many stock photos with exaggerated smiles

Pages to design:

Homepage

Large hero section with trust-building headline

Short explanation of the service

Key benefits (24h care, legal employment, experienced caregivers)

Process overview (3–4 steps)

Testimonials

Contact call-to-action

Services Page

Explanation of 24-hour care

Daily support examples (cooking, companionship, hygiene assistance)

Who the service is suitable for

How It Works

Step-by-step explanation of the placement process

Timeline from request to caregiver arrival

About Us

Hanseatic values: reliability, honesty, long-term relationships

Local and personal service approach

Contact Page

Simple contact form

Phone number prominently displayed

Optional consultation request

UX considerations:

Very clear navigation

Large buttons and spacing

Senior-friendly readability

Mobile-first responsive layout

The final design should feel trustworthy, calm, human, and regionally grounded in Northern Germany.
